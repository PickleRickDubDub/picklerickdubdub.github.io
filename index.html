Welcome one and welcome all to the only place where you can relax, or sharpen your dueling skills!


This resort was created specifically for duelists who need to relax, but at the same time, never forget that they are chasing a goal.

With 2 different styles of rooms and full access to the arena, we can make sure that everyone's needs are being met at all times

Battle City Inn is currently only hosting a small number of dedicated duelists. If this takes off, Seto Kaiba himself will begin the expansion of this new project all around the globe, thank you for your participation. We offer..



  1. Well-lit areas with ample table space for card layouts and side decks, ensuring a comfortable dueling experience. Battle stations and custom-themed duel rooms for an immersive touch.
  2. Robust internet connectivity to check card databases or use the official Yu-Gi-Oh! Neuron app to manage Life Points and track matches.
  3. High-quality, ergonomic chairs for long hours of play.
  4. Easily accessible and affordable food/snack options, or visit our cafeteria, fully stocked with a vast variety of local food and dishes.
  5. Special duelist packages and discounted group rates.
  6. Designate safe, well-lit public areas for players to trade cards with plenty of security guards at all point to ensure safety at all times.
  7. Great capacity to host in-house tournaments or locals, with potential perks like store credit or booster packs as prizes.
  8. Each room has its very own safe with customisable codes to ensure the safety of every guest's decks and cards.
