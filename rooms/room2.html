Comfort like no other with a wonderful view in this room personally approved by Seto Kaiba himself. Starting at just $300 per night, this room will welcome you with the upmost respect for an elite duelist.


Champion room features.
  1. Champion rooms are larger than standard rooms with the addition of a kitchen, providing ample space to move around and include a distinct sitting area with a sofa, armchair and a coffee table.
  2. The private en-suite bathrooms feature a combination of a walk-in shower and a separate deep-soaking bathtub and rain shower head. They also come with high-end, branded toiletries, plush bathrobes, and slippers.
  3. Beyond standard coffee/tea facilities, champion rooms include a well-stocked mini-bar with a curated selection of premium snacks and beverages, and an espresso or Nespresso machine.
  4. Expect technology upgrades like a larger flat-screen smart TV with streaming capabilities, high-speed Wi-Fi, ample power outlets, and a Bluetooth speaker with smart room controls for lighting and temperature.
  5. These rooms are located on higher floors, positioned within the hotel to offer better views, such as a city skyline, ocean, and garden, and include a private balcony with large panoramic windows.
